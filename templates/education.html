Blood Types & Compatibility

Understanding your blood type and who you can donate to.

A+ Blood Type

Can donate to: A+, AB+

Can receive from: A+, A-, O+, O-

B+ Blood Type

Can donate to: B+, AB+

Can receive from: B+, B-, O+, O-

O+ Blood Type

Can donate to: O+, A+, B+, AB+

Can receive from: O+, O-

AB+ Blood Type

Can donate to: AB+

Can receive from: All types

Common Myths

Debunking misconceptions about blood donation.

  • Myth: Donating blood makes you weak. Fact: Your body replaces the blood within 24 hours.
  • Myth: You can get diseases from donating. Fact: Sterile equipment is used for each donor.
  • Myth: Only certain people can donate. Fact: Most healthy adults can donate.

Health Benefits

Why donating blood is good for you.

  • Reduces risk of heart disease
  • Helps maintain healthy iron levels
  • Stimulates blood cell production
  • Provides free health screening
